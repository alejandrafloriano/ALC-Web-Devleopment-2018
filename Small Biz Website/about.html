About Crazy Bob!

Crazy Bob has been farming all his life. Ever since he was little, his favorite thing to grow were pumpkins. In 1978, Bob decided to open up a small stand where he could sell a couple of his pumpkins, and eventually his small stand turned into the buisness you see today. Crazy Bob's love of all things Fall and Halloween have helped shaped the company along the way.
